# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making NexusAI-Gateway a respectful, inclusive, and productive environment for contributors, maintainers, and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all communication.
- Focus feedback on code, architecture, and product outcomes.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating concerns.
- Respect differing viewpoints and implementation approaches.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Deliberate sabotage of code, reviews, or collaboration.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or private project information in public channels.
- Dismissing security, privacy, or accessibility concerns without review.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all repository-related communication and collaboration, including issues, pull requests, review comments, and associated discussion channels.
